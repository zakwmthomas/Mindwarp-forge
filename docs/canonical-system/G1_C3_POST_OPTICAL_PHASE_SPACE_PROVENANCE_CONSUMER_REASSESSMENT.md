# G1 / C3 post optical phase-space provenance consumer reassessment

Date: 2026-07-17

Status: **the additive cell owner closes source measure, ancestry and affine
correlation, but a coupling consumer is not implementation-ready because no
verified subject proves that the six forms are the transported image of the
cell through one uniform physical/interface topology.**

## What is now closed

`optical-phase-space-cell-binding` supplies the subjects that the prior
whole-cell oracle lacked:

- one consumer-independent four-symbol parent and exact positive measure;
- nonduplicating binary ancestry with exact child-measure conservation;
- six fixed-order correlated affine output forms with an outward remainder;
- strict identities and Q160/Q1.62 directed projections; and
- typed depth, arithmetic, codec and authority failure.

The former current-owner reuse/mutation gap is therefore closed. A future
consumer can name one complete cell without changing physical, interface,
lineage, cumulative-transfer or receiver owners.

## Surviving blocker

The cell owner deliberately binds a declaration; it makes no claim that its
forms are physically correct. Its identity contains no physical-volume recipe,
bulk profile, interface sequence, band/time basis, ordered lineage topology,
branch certificate or receiver ordering surface.

The existing point/interval owners can replay local boxes and branches, but
they do not derive a shared-symbol affine map from the source cell through an
arbitrary ordered lineage. The receiver owner intentionally rejects
nondegenerate conditional boxes. Consequently a coupling consumer that
accepted only the cell and projection receipt could certify full/zero arrival
from caller-declared, physically unproved forms. That would turn provenance
into transport authority and is rejected.

## Smallest next proof

Design and test, without production source, one
`optical-phase-space-transport-certificate` mathematical subject. It must bind:

- the complete phase-space `cell_id`, scope and reconstruction;
- explicit same-band and time basis;
- physical-volume/profile identities and one ordered topology/branch family;
- a derivation transcript from the input symbols to all six output forms;
- outward remainder growth at every free-space and planar-interface step;
- typed topology change, branch ambiguity, fold/caustic possibility,
  conditional widening and unsupported nonlinear operation; and
- an authority-negative result that cannot claim arrival or coupling.

The oracle must retain the existing full/zero/unresolved portfolios while
adding adversarial forged-form, wrong-cell, wrong-band/time, reordered-step,
branch drift, TIR boundary, fold and remainder-understatement cases. A free
space affine family should be exact. Planar same-branch refraction may survive
only with independently checked outward rational enclosures; otherwise it
must remain typed unsupported.

## Stop boundary

This reassessment authorizes mathematical design and a disposable exact oracle
only. Add no crate, contract schema, dependency, production test or source.
Do not modify any current owner. Do not claim coupling, source magnitude,
spreading, received power, detector response, visibility, runtime, promotion
or C3 closure. Any code-facing transport-certificate package remains a later
readiness audit and separate serious owner decision.
