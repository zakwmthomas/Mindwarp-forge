## Alternatives and decision

1. **Automatically derive ecology from physical opportunity** — rejected.
   This launders physical evidence into habitat, resource, hazard, trophic,
   competition or occupancy truth.
2. **Trust caller labels or edges as facts** — rejected. Labels and graph
   closure do not establish ecological truth or provenance.
3. **Versioned authored candidate assertions** — selected for separate
   design/readiness. They must carry exact world/subject/evidence provenance,
   be explicitly authored-candidate or hypothesis, preserve
   unavailable/indeterminate, and validate only checkable necessary
   conditions.
4. **Realized ecological truth** — deferred and evidence-blocked at this
   stage. Later physiology, realized-occupancy and population packages own it.

The selected seam may describe abstract candidate roles such as habitat
requirement, resource requirement, hazard exposure candidate, trophic
candidate, competition candidate and prospective occupancy. It may not ship
product vocabulary or claim that any candidate is satisfied, viable, occupied
or biologically true.
