# G1 / C3 post optical phase-space transport consumer reassessment

Date: 2026-07-17

Status: **the verified transport certificate closes same-medium ordered-face
derivation and can be replayed as upstream whole-cell evidence, but general
whole-cell receiver coupling remains blocked because no subject binds receiver
geometry and strict receiver-before-face ordering to the transported cell.**

## Decision

The new `optical-phase-space-transport-certificate` is a valid upstream input
for a future whole-cell coupling consumer. It binds one measured correlated
source cell, one nonzero band/time identity, one unchanged physical volume and
an ordered run of uniformly certified same-medium face events. Each face form
is derived directly from the immutable origin, is contained by the physical
owner's receipt, and retains shared-symbol correlation.

That closes the prior physical-derivation gap. It does not make the abstract
full/zero/unresolved whole-cell classifier implementation-ready. Transport
evidence is deliberately receiver-independent and owns no receiver ordering
surface, receiver identity, accepted measure, arrival or coupling result.

## Macro fit

The architecture remains additive and acyclic:

`phase-space cell -> origin-anchored same-medium transport -> future coupling
consumer -> optional later power or visibility consumers`

The cell continues to own source measure and partition ancestry. The transport
certificate owns only physical same-medium face derivation. The existing
receiver owner continues to own strict exact-ray arrival. A future whole-cell
consumer would have to replay these owners without changing their V1 bytes or
claiming their authority.

This separation is preferable to importing the phase-space cell into the
physical, lineage or exact-ray receiver owners. It preserves existing identity
domains and deletion rollback, and it allows one transported source partition
to be compared with multiple receivers without changing source or transport
identity.

## Micro evidence now available

The verified certificate supplies all of the following without trust in a
caller-declared transported form:

- exact positive source-cell measure and binary partition ancestry through the
  replayed cell;
- four shared symbols and outward residual intervals;
- a nonzero `band_time_id`;
- physical scope, reconstruction, volume and current-cell replay;
- up to 64 uniformly selected same-medium cell faces in exact order;
- three correlated position forms at every certified face, derived from the
  immutable origin rather than a reduced predecessor;
- conservative Q160 projections contained by unchanged physical receipts; and
- strict identity, codec, work, arithmetic and authority-negative receipts.

For the special mathematical case where the receiver ordering surface is the
certified face plane, those position forms are sufficient evidence for a
conservative face-footprint full/zero/unresolved comparison. That observation
does not establish arrival: receiver openness, depth, ordering and contact
rules still belong to a consumer.

## Remaining general coupling gap

An axis-aligned receiver can lie inside a physical cell before the next
certified face. The certificate does not bind that receiver, solve the
shared-symbol receiver entry time, or prove that entry occurs before the
physical face for the complete cell. Its terminal at a medium change also
provides no reflected or refracted branch. Consequently the following remain
unproved:

1. one receiver identity and positive-volume open geometry in the same scope;
2. a conservative correlated image at each required receiver ordering surface;
3. strict whole-cell receiver-before-face ordering, including boundary contact
   and partial overlap;
4. uniformity of the selected receiver/face ordering across the complete
   parameter cell;
5. exact accepted, zero and unresolved measure conservation under subdivision;
   and
6. typed stops for ordering ambiguity, interface requirement, projection
   widening, fold possibility and work exhaustion.

The current exact-ray receiver cannot supply this by reuse: it intentionally
rejects nondegenerate point or direction evidence. The transport certificate
cannot supply it by reinterpretation: doing so would turn receiver-independent
face evidence into coupling and arrival authority.

## Adopt / adapt / build decision

| Route | Decision |
|---|---|
| Treat every certified face footprint as receiver arrival | **Reject.** A physical face is not a receiver and contact/order semantics are absent. |
| Feed nondegenerate forms to the exact-ray receiver | **Reject.** That changes its proven degenerate-ray contract. |
| Collapse forms to independent boxes | **Fallback only.** It may widen to unresolved but cannot recover correlation or certify general ordering. |
| Reinterpret the transport terminal as zero or darkness | **Reject.** Every terminal is authority-negative. |
| Design a separate whole-cell receiver-coupling consumer that replays cell, transport and receiver evidence | **Select for code-free mathematical design and disposable exact oracle only.** |

## Smallest next proof

The next bounded package should refine the existing abstract whole-cell
classifier into a receiver-ordering design over one certified same-medium
free-space segment. It should reuse the frozen strict full/zero/unresolved and
measure-conservation semantics, then prove or type:

- receiver-before-face for the complete correlated cell;
- receiver-after-face and closed-disjoint zero cases;
- equality, partial overlap and mixed ordering as unresolved;
- the face-coincident special case without promoting contact;
- subdivision conservation across 4, 16 and 64 children;
- correlation-erasure fallback that can only widen to unresolved; and
- interface, ordering, fold and resource terminals that retain unresolved
  measure.

The cheapest sufficient tier is static exact-rational design followed, only if
needed, by a disposable oracle. No crate, schema, dependency, production test
or source is authorized. A surviving code-facing consumer package would be a
materially new owner and must return through readiness and an explicit owner
decision before implementation.

## Authority and stop boundary

This reassessment grants no coupling, arrival, accepted fraction, source
magnitude, attenuation, spreading, received power, detector response,
visibility, perception, runtime, promotion or C3-closure authority. Existing
cell, transport, physical, lineage, cumulative and receiver owners remain
unchanged.

Stop before source if the receiver-ordering proof needs to mutate an existing
owner, assumes an interface branch, estimates partial coverage, loses
unresolved measure, or cannot retain exact identity and bounded arithmetic.
