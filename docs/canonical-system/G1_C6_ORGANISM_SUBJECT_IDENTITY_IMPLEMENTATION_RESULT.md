## Implemented result

The capability-free `organism-subject-identity` crate owns seven strict typed
records: lineage subject, form template, unresolved species candidate, stable
individual, mutable individual-subject binding, population identity and
individual-targeted lifecycle/history binding. A private-field, non-encoded
bundle proves that these records were reconstructed through their production
validators rather than assembled by a downstream consumer.

Stable individual identity is separate from reusable form identity and mutable
classification/binding state. The lifecycle binding replays the exact C4
known-good history for that individual and rejects corrupt, partial, foreign or
over-budget evidence. Species and population records remain identity envelopes;
they do not assert membership, members, counts or distribution.

`person-form-eligibility` is the one production consumer. Its additive public
entry point requires the validated bundle plus the exact body-plan family and
expression, then delegates the legacy five-dimension report unchanged. It does
not decide personhood, comparison, compatibility, capacity truth or eligibility.
