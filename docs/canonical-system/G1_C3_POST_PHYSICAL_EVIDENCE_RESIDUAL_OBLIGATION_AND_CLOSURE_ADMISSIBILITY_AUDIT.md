## Closure-admissibility result

C3 cannot honestly close under the current master contract. In particular:

1. `unavailable_evidence` is a valid scientific result, not satisfied physical
   applicability.
2. Abstract Q32.32 coordinates and per-coordinate-unit coefficients cannot be
   relabelled as SI truth.
3. A swept generic probe does not prove walkability, support, locomotion or
   organism-relative traversal.
4. A physical-region identity is not a biome and cannot paint a visible seam.
5. Dimensionless transfer, positive coupling and source energy do not prove
   received energy, detector response or perception.
6. C3 may not wait on C6 for an observer-independent obligation while C6 waits
   on C3; such a cycle must be resolved explicitly rather than hidden.
7. Cross-compilation and same-host replay are not independent-device promotion
   evidence.

Closing C3 would therefore require either authoritative physical evidence or
an explicit owner-approved master-scope revision reconciled across every
canonical dependency and closure surface. This audit authorizes neither.

half of the master ecotone rule. It must define and falsify fixtures for:

  averaged away;

  and downstream biome influence;

  similarity or biome score; and

The audit must compare at least direct categorical painting, fixed-width
blending, cause-scaled mixing and evidence-preserving typed boundaries. It may
select a mathematical fixture model and name an independent oracle, but it may
not add a crate, contract schema, production source, renderer, biome, organism,
terrain, runtime behavior, coefficient catalogue, physical calibration,
promotion or C3 closure.
