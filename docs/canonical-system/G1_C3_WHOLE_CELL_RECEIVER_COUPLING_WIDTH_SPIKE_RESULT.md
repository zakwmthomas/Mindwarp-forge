## Rejected representation

An independently reduced public `TransportAffineFormV1` scalar may consume the
upstream 490-bit live shield. A single product of two such terms therefore has
a 980-bit upper bound before addition or reduction. This route cannot use
`Signed512` safely and must not be implemented.

## Surviving representation

Replay the transport input and selected physical face from the immutable
64-bit common-denominator origin. Do not multiply public reduced direct forms.

The physical-volume constraints bound a receiver Q160 numerator to 224 bits
and a physical Q32 face numerator to 96 bits. For common origin denominator
`D`, point numerator `p`, receiver numerator `R` and physical face numerator
`H`, retain:

- receiver plane numerator `R*D - p*2^160`: 289-bit centre and 224-bit
  coefficients;
- physical plane numerator `H*D - p*2^32`: 161-bit centre and 96-bit
  coefficients;
- receiver-before-face quadratic bound: **391 bits**; and
- receiver cross-axis quadratic bound: **359 bits**.

The maximum is therefore **391 bits**, leaving **121 bits** below the 512-bit
storage boundary. The bound combines like correlated monomials before exact
termwise interval evaluation and assumes no favourable gcd cancellation.
