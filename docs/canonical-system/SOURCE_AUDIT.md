- project-scoped Cursor transcript stores for the Quantum Tunnel repository and
  its `Assets/Scripts` subtree, containing owner ideas, corrections,
  play observations, worker prompts and delegated-agent records; and
- Devin logs and resulting files, but no locally retained Devin session
  history in the inspected database.

These sources are now routed through
`MIND_WARP_GAMEPLAY_FOUNDATION_RECOVERY_MAP.md`. They remain evidence only.
Main Mind Warp, the smaller Quantum Tunnel product, Forge and the historical
automation/control-room system must remain distinct contexts.

The owner has explicitly rejected reuse of historical code. Recovery is
clean-room gameplay design: concepts and observed problems may be adjudicated,
but code, schemas, architecture, numeric balance and old completion claims may
not be imported.

## Evidence rule

Each package is classified as source evidence until its relevant test can be
re-run, its inputs/artifacts can be inspected, and its result is recorded in a
new engine-neutral proof pack. Python timings, hand-authored visual rigs, and
old reports do not establish runtime-engine performance or end-to-end asset
generation.
