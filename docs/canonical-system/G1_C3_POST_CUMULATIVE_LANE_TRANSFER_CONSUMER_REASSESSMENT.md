## What the result closes

Forge can now replay one complete spectral lane and derive the exact ordered
bulk and followed-interface factors into a cumulative dimensionless enclosure.
This closes the numerical fold gap without changing any local owner and without
turning opacity, underflow or termination into darkness or visibility.

It does not say that any emitter launched energy into the lane, that the lane
intersects a receiver before its next certified face, that distance spreading
has been evaluated, or that a detector responds. Those are independent facts
and must not be collapsed into the cumulative coefficient.

## Why geometry comes before source magnitude

A source quantity cannot prove that the followed lane reaches a receiver. Two
records may have identical source emission and cumulative transfer while one
receiver lies inside the first traversed cell and another lies outside every
step. Multiplying either record first would produce a bounded number with no
arrival subject.

Receiver geometry can be tested independently with the existing physical
coordinate frame. Each step already carries an initial point box, a declared
conditional direction box and, when certified, a next-face hit box. A future
query may bind an explicit bounded receiver shape and ask only whether an
intersection is certified before the step boundary. That proof can remain
unitless and authority-negative; source emission can compose later.

## Mandatory counterexamples for the next audit

The mathematical audit must distinguish at least:

- receiver wholly before the next face versus wholly after it;
- tangent contact versus strict interior entry;
- start-inside versus first positive-parameter entry;
- a receiver crossing a cell boundary at the same parameter as the face;
- interval direction boxes for which some rays hit and others miss;
- two receivers with overlapping parameter intervals and no unique first hit;
- an unavailable, ambiguous, no-progress or interface-terminal step;
- outer-exit and work-exhaustion terminals that are not themselves receivers;
- receiver identity, coordinate-frame, reconstruction and lineage substitution;
- deleted, duplicated, reordered or independently resealed step evidence;
- cap, arithmetic, limitation and authority mutation; and
- any attempt to infer aperture, orientation, detector response, visibility or
  darkness from geometric contact.

The audit must compare an exact rational reference with a bounded checked
fixed-width construction, state whether the existing conditional direction box
is sufficient for universal hit/miss classification, and reject implementation
if interval dependency makes the proposed certificate unsound.
