## Decision

The implemented conditional cell step closes the local geometry prerequisite:
for one declared point/direction box it either certifies one strictly earliest
face with containing time and hit-point boxes, or returns typed ambiguity,
zero progress, outer exit or unavailable-neighbour evidence. Existing
exact-path V1 bytes and identities remain locked.

The smallest dependency-correct next package is a **one-band conditional
interval bulk-transfer mathematical and oracle audit**. It may consume a
validated cell-step input/event and a separately validated bulk profile, but
must remain a local conditional calculation. It cannot bind a sequence,
claim optical lineage, select an endpoint, call another interface or compose a
visible path.

End-to-end composition remains premature because no owner yet binds:

1. the initial exact face point to the first conditional point box;
2. an interface band's transmitted direction to the matching cell-step input;
3. each cell-step output point to the next local input;
4. attenuation receipts to the same ordered band lane; or
5. termination and endpoint relation across a bounded sequence.

Those are composer responsibilities after every local numerical operation is
available. Putting them into interval bulk would create a second composer by
stealth.

## Why the interval bulk surface must be one band

The first exact path can share geometry across RGB, but a smooth interface can
emit different transmitted direction boxes for red, green and blue. After
that event, the three bands can reach different next faces and traverse
different media. A three-band bulk call over one shared conditional cell step
would silently re-correlate dispersed paths.

The selected candidate therefore evaluates exactly one spectral band and one
cell-step receipt per call. A later composer may maintain up to three ordered
lanes and aggregate their work, but the local bulk owner never assumes shared
post-interface geometry.

## Length semantics: normalization cannot be assumed

`ConditionalIntervalCellStepInputV1` deliberately makes no unit-vector claim;
it accepts any ordered Q1.62 direction box within component limits, including
zero-containing states. Therefore its Q160 face time is a path parameter, not
automatically Euclidean length.

The audit must compare at least these sound candidates:

1. **speed-norm times face-time enclosure:** outwardly enclose
   `sqrt(dx^2 + dy^2 + dz^2) * t` over the complete direction and certified
   time boxes;
2. **endpoint-displacement enclosure:** subtract the input point box from the
   certified hit-point box and outwardly enclose the Euclidean norm; and
3. **correlated richer state:** retain point/direction/time correlation only if
   it has a fixed replayable size and measured advantage.

The first candidate is the leading baseline because constant-direction motion
has exact length `|d|t` without a normalization assumption. Interval
dependency between speed and exit time makes the independent product wider but
not unsound. The endpoint candidate is an independent cross-check and may
collapse its lower bound through correlation erasure. Neither may narrow by a
midpoint, corner or asserted unit vector.

If ordinary admitted fixtures commonly produce a zero lower length or unusably
wide optical-depth bounds, the candidate fails utility even when sound.

## Local ownership and typed outcomes

An interval bulk extension belongs with `visible-radiance-bulk-transfer`
because that module already owns substance profiles, extinction coefficients,
optical depth, transmission kernels and bulk-specific outcomes. It may depend
on validated physical-path cell-step objects; it may not copy cell traversal.

The audit must distinguish:

- known vacuum identity;
- known finite one-band attenuation bounds;
- known opaque termination;
- unavailable current-cell evidence;
- upstream ambiguous or no-forward-progress geometry;
- certified span followed by unavailable neighbour or outer exit; and
- arithmetic ceiling or nonconvergence defects.

An unavailable *neighbour* does not erase attenuation already certified
through the available current cell. An unavailable *current cell* cannot be
treated as vacuum. Outer exit likewise terminates only after the current-cell
span receipt is retained.

## Arithmetic consolidation decision

`physical-path-substrate` and `visible-radiance-interface-event` now contain
similar private checked signed-512 and directed-division machinery. Blindly
copying a third implementation into interval bulk would turn every arithmetic
repair into three opportunities for drift.

The selected audit must inventory the exact common operations required by all
three owners and compare:

1. retain private implementations behind independent identity locks;
2. extract a capability-free target-neutral fixed-arithmetic crate and migrate
   existing owners under byte/ID compatibility fixtures; or
3. expose a narrower stable arithmetic primitive without exposing native
   limbs or semantic policy.

No extraction is presumed. A shared module is selected only if the concrete
third-use operation set and reduced maintenance risk outweigh the refactor and
platform blast radius. Production readiness must reject a third copy without
an explicit documented disposition.

## Exact next audit

The mathematical/oracle audit must answer:

1. What minimum identities bind one bulk profile, band, current cell,
   cell-step input and certified event without nesting unbounded history?
2. Which typed geometry outcomes admit a positive span, and which retain a
   current-cell transfer before terminal neighbour evidence?
3. Can fixed checked 512-bit arithmetic enclose speed norm, length, optical
   depth and transmission at one frozen precision and work ceiling?
4. Does speed-time agree with exact correlated `Fraction` witnesses for every
   admitted corner and generated interior state?
5. How much false width comes from direction/time correlation erasure over
   normal, near-parallel, face-critical, negative-coordinate, maximum-range
   and 64-step dispersed lanes?
6. Can the existing bulk exponential kernel be reused inside its owning crate
   without changing exact bulk V1 bytes or duplicating numerical policy?
7. What maximum input/event bytes, live arithmetic bits, divisions, roots,
   series terms and allocation sizes are required?
8. Which arithmetic consolidation disposition prevents a third divergent
   signed-512 implementation?

The oracle must include finite-zero, finite-positive and opaque coefficients;
vacuum and unavailable current cells; all face directions; minimum-positive
Q1.62 motion; zero-straddling and correlation-erased boxes; outer and
unavailable-neighbour terminations; exact versus nonsquare norm cases;
coefficient extremes; and at least four 64-step per-band lanes.
