# Owner Decision Brief Contract v0.1

An owner brief is a read-only, bounded summary intended to reduce solo-creator
cognitive load. It surfaces at most five proposed candidates that may need an
explicit future decision.

The brief must state that it is not approval, promotion, or a command queue.
It cannot mutate the ledger, hide additional decisions, execute code, or infer
owner authority from imported text. Every listed item links only to durable
candidate/evidence identifiers and lifecycle state.
