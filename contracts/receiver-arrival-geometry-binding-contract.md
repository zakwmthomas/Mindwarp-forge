# Receiver-arrival geometry binding contract

Status: additive capability-free prototype under the explicitly approved G1/C3 package.

The owner accepts one validated optical lineage bundle and manifest plus one
positive-volume Cartesian Q160 AABB bound to the same physical scope and
reconstruction. It replays every upstream object before classifying geometry.

Only exact degenerate point, direction and required face-time intervals are
admitted. Nondegenerate evidence produces typed
`unsupported_conditional_evidence`; no midpoint, corner, representative ray,
epsilon or favourable witness is used.

Strict arrival is open-slab intersection under `0 <= t < t_face` in lineage
order. Start-inside is explicit. Tangent, point contact and a receiver/face tie
are contact evidence, never current-step arrival. Upstream outcomes without a
certified face remain typed terminals.

All arithmetic uses checked signed-512 values under the 414-bit live shield.
The owner admits at most 64 steps, 384 directed divisions, 768 comparisons and
64 three-axis intersections. Strict reconstruct-and-compare codecs cap input,
output and live canonical validation at 18 MiB, 256 KiB and 32 MiB.

The result has `none_evidence_only` authority. It establishes no source
emission, spreading, received power, aperture, detector response, visibility,
detectability, darkness, perception, rendering, gameplay line of sight,
runtime, persistence, promotion or C3 closure.
