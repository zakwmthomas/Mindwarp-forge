# Optical Lane Transfer Binding Contract v1

This additive capability-free contract consumes one complete validated
`optical-lineage-binding` bundle and manifest. The compiler replays that owner
before deriving any numerical factor. Callers cannot submit factors, cumulative
endpoints, a terminal, work receipt, result identity, transcript identity,
limitations, or authority effect.

For each ordinal with a `known_current_cell_transfer`, the compiler selects the
same-band bulk transfer: vacuum is exact one, finite uses the owner's Q0.48
endpoints, and opaque is exact zero. The three upstream terminal outcomes with
no evaluated current-cell transfer contribute no factor and never become zero.
Only `continue_after_interface` adds a second factor, taken from the selected
band's owner-produced all-transmit event. Terminal interfaces and same-medium
continuations add no interface factor. Every receipt binds ordinal, role, band,
owner object identity, endpoints, and a separate factor-domain identity.

The accumulator starts at `2^160`. Every ordered Q0.48 factor applies
`L' = floor(L*a/2^48)` and `U' = ceil(U*b/2^48)` using checked signed-512
storage from `fixed-interval-arithmetic`. Live multiplication values may use at
most 209 bits. At most 128 factors are admitted. Projection to public Q0.48 is
performed outward exactly once after the fold. `[0,1]` is a valid underflow
enclosure; `[0,0]` requires an owner-produced exact-zero factor and has no
darkness meaning.

Input, output, and conservative live canonical bytes are capped at 18 MiB,
256 KiB, and 32 MiB. Strict JSON codecs reject unknown fields, trailing or
noncanonical bytes, foreign or stale nested evidence, reordered or substituted
factors, forged endpoints and identities, limitation drift, and authority
mutation. Validation recompiles and compares the entire result.

The copied ten-family lineage terminal is not reclassified. The output is only
a dimensionless cumulative followed-lane transfer enclosure. It makes no claim
of source emission, inverse-square spreading, receiver geometry or arrival,
aperture or orientation, detector response, detectability, visibility,
perception, rendering, gameplay line of sight, runtime, persistence, approval,
promotion, or C3 closure. Its authority effect is exactly
`none_evidence_only`.
